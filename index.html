About Us

We started our journey on 24th March 2021, located in Ludhiana, Punjab, India. PAWAN KNITWEAR specializes in producing high-quality knitted and crocheted woolen fabrics.





Our Machinery

We use advanced knitting machines to produce high-quality knitted and crocheted woolen fabrics. These machines are equipped with state-of-the-art technology to ensure precision and efficiency in our manufacturing process.
